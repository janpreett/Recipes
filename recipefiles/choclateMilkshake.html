Choclate Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1/4 cup chocolate syrup, plus extra for drizzling
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, and 1/4 cup of chocolate syrup in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Drizzle chocolate syrup inside a tall glass.
  4. Pour the chocolate milkshake into the glass.
  5. Top with whipped cream (optional).
