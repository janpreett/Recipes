Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water (110째F to 115째F)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 pound low-moisture whole-milk mozzarella, grated
  • 1 28-ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Cornmeal (for dusting)
  • Crushed garlic, red pepper flakes, fresh basil, and grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine the bread flour, salt, sugar, and active dry yeast in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add lukewarm water and 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix with a spoon until a rough dough forms.
  3. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 to 7 minutes, adding more flour if needed, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise for 2 hours at room temperature until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 500째F (260째C) with a pizza stone or heavy baking sheet inside.
  6. Divide the dough in half and roll out each piece on a floured surface into a 12- to 14-inch round.
  7. Spread half of the crushed tomatoes over the rolled dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  8. Sprinkle half of the grated mozzarella over the tomato sauce.
  9. Add any optional toppings such as crushed garlic, red pepper flakes, fresh basil, and grated Parmesan.
  10. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet
  11. Bake for about 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is nicely melted and slightly browned.
  12. Repeat the process with the remaining dough, sauce, and cheese.
